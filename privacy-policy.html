Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Prometheus Hunk (“we”, “our”, “us”) operates automated systems that help publish gaming-related content—including videos, clips, posts, thumbnails, and updates—across various platforms such as YouTube, X (Twitter), Facebook, Instagram, Threads, Telegram, and other services used by the brand.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information from users of public content. However, our systems may access:

  • Platform authentication tokens (e.g., YouTube, Telegram, X, Meta).
  • Account IDs and metadata required to publish content.
  • Content you upload such as videos, titles, descriptions, thumbnails.
  • Logs related to publishing (timestamps, platform response status, errors).

2. How We Use Information

We process the above information strictly for:

  • Uploading videos, images, posts, and reels automatically.
  • Scheduling and managing content across platforms.
  • Generating AI-assisted captions, descriptions, or transformations.
  • Moderation, troubleshooting, and improving automation reliability.

3. What We Do NOT Collect

  • No passwords.
  • No payment information.
  • No private messages.
  • No personal demographic data.

4. How We Store & Protect Data

Access tokens and secrets are securely stored in Google Cloud Secret Manager with strict access controls. No sensitive credentials are ever shared publicly.

5. Third-Party Services

We interact with these APIs only to publish content:

  • YouTube Data API
  • Meta Graph API (Facebook, Instagram, Threads)
  • X API
  • Telegram Bot API
  • Tumblr API
  • Other platforms added in the future

Each service is governed by their own privacy policies. We do not access more information than required to publish content.

6. Cookies

This website (GitHub Pages hosted) does not use cookies for tracking or analytics. GitHub may use essential cookies for hosting functionality.

7. Children’s Privacy

Our services are not designed for individuals under 13. We do not knowingly process children’s data.

8. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as the platform grows. The “Last updated” date will always reflect the latest version.
